# 🚨 E-Commerce Revenue Leakage & Customer Churn Analytics

> Tracking revenue is easy. Understanding where it leaks is the real challenge.

This project analyzes how e-commerce businesses lose revenue through churn, cancellations, and operational inefficiencies using Power BI and SQL.

---

## 💼 About

**Vanu Analytics** builds data solutions to detect revenue loss, reduce churn, and improve profitability.

---

## 📊 Key Metrics

- Total Revenue: 16.01M  
- Gross Revenue: 13.59M  
- Net Revenue: 11.34M  
- Revenue Leakage: ~2.25M  
- Customers: 99K  
- Churn Customers: 96K ⚠️  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Revenue drop from Gross → Net indicates leakage risk  
- 96% churn → weak retention strategy  
- Late deliveries → cancellations → revenue loss  
- High dependency on credit cards → payment risk  
- Top regions (SP, RJ, MG) drive both revenue & cost  

---

## 📉 Revenue Loss

- Cancelled Revenue: 95K+  
- Churn = major future revenue risk  

---

## 🧠 Recommendations

- Improve delivery performance  
- Strengthen customer retention  
- Diversify payment methods  
- Optimize high-cost regions
